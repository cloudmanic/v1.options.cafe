Have questions? We have answers!

Can I keep using the free account?

We offer a free trial because we believe that you will see that Options Cafe is a valuable tool that will help you make better trades. After 30 days you are required to update to the paid account.

What if I change my mind?

You can cancel any time. We also offer a generous refund for annual accounts, we want you to be happy!

Will you keep making updates and enhancements to the application?

Of course. Options Cafe is always improving. If there is some feature you want to see please contact us at help@options.cafe. We love feedback.

What is the deal with Tradier Brokerage?

We have partnered with Tradier Brokerage to provide broker services for Options Cafe. As an Options Cafe customer you will receive unlimited commission-free trades with Tradier Brokerage. Some additional information on how to setup a Tradier Brokerage account can be found here.
